About The Event

About us

About us Content

Welcome to the Intervarsity Corporate - Academia Symposium (ICAS'24), a dynamic platform where academia and industry converge to explore the forefront of knowledge exchange and collaboration. Organized by Sunway Diploma Studies in collaboration with ASLI (Asian Strategy & Leadership Institute) and SBS (Sunway Business School), this symposium stands as a beacon of innovation and insight in the realm of corporate academia engagement.

Our Mission

At ICAS'24, our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and the corporate world, fostering meaningful dialogue, mutual understanding, and collaboration. We believe that by bringing together diverse perspectives from both sectors, we can drive innovation, address contemporary challenges, and pave the way for a brighter future.

What We Offer

Through a series of thought-provoking keynote speeches, engaging panel discussions, and interactive workshops, ICAS'24 offers participants the opportunity to delve into key issues facing academia and industry today. From emerging trends in business and technology to the impact of globalization and sustainability, our symposium covers a wide range of topics designed to inspire, educate, and empower.

Why Attend

Whether you are an academic seeking to enhance your research impact, or a business leader interested in tapping into the latest academic knowledge, ICAS'24 provides a unique platform for networking, learning, and collaboration. By participating in our symposium, you will have the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals, exchange ideas, and explore new opportunities for collaboration and growth.

Who Should Attend

ICAS'24 welcomes participants from academia, industry, government, and the wider community who are passionate about driving positive change through collaboration and innovation. Whether you are a researcher, educator, entrepreneur, or business leader, there is something for everyone at ICAS'24.

Join Us

We invite you to join us at ICAS'24 and be part of this exciting journey towards greater collaboration between academia and the corporate world. Together, we can unlock new opportunities, drive innovation, and create a better future for all.
